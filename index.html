Hi. I'm Steve.
And that's Zeke. My farmdog.

social worker, farmer, and photographer

After seven incredible years of living and working in Hawaii as a juvenile mental health counselor, I moved to Washington state to pursue a life of living rural and growing produce and raising livestock. Over there on the right is my farmdog and best friend, Zeke. He's been with me almost every day of his life, and we've had many adventures together, from college with me in Southern California to surfing the beautiful waves of Hawaii, to now helping me hunt down chicken-eating raccoons in our back yard forest, and growing over 60 varieties of fruits and vegetables, many of which are given to the needy in our local community. Over the years he's taught me some of the most important life-lessons I've learned: perserverance, loyalty, and living in the moment.

Lately I've been learning full-stack development in order to get a thrilling job in the tech industry in nearby Seattle. These new skills, combined with Wordpress, photography, and graphic design I've learned over the years will hopefully one day help me realize my dream of being a full-time web development professional.
